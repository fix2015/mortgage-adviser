## Production Notes

### Brand Guidelines for All Videos

- **Primary colour:** Emerald Green (#059669) -- backgrounds, UI elements, positive indicators
- **Secondary colour:** Warm Gold (#D97706) -- accents, highlights, price points, premium feel
- **Typography:** Use a clean sans-serif (Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans, or similar) for overlays. Avoid decorative fonts.
- **Logo placement:** Bottom-right corner watermark throughout, full logo in CTA frames
- **Tone:** Warm, trustworthy, modern, slightly aspirational. Never pushy or salesy.

### Deliverable Checklist

| Format | Aspect Ratio | Resolution | Use Case |
|--------|-------------|------------|----------|
| Vertical | 9:16 | 1080x1920 | TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts |
| Landscape | 16:9 | 1920x1080 | YouTube, website embed, presentations |
| Square | 1:1 | 1080x1080 | LinkedIn, Facebook feed, Instagram feed |

### File Naming Convention

```
MortgageAI_[VideoName]_[AspectRatio]_[Version].mp4

Examples:
MortgageAI_ProductDemo_9x16_v1.mp4
MortgageAI_HeroExplainer_16x9_v1.mp4
MortgageAI_BrokerVsAI_1x1_v1.mp4
```

### Subtitles

All videos must include:
- Burned-in captions (for social autoplay with sound off)
- Separate .srt file for each aspect ratio
- Font: Bold sans-serif, white with dark shadow/outline for readability
- Position: Lower third, avoiding logo watermark

### Music and Sound

- All music must be royalty-free or properly licensed
- Sound effects: subtle UI sounds (clicks, chimes, whooshes) to reinforce interactions
- Voiceover must be recorded professionally (British accent, RP or light regional)
- Mix levels: voiceover at -6dB, music at -18dB, sound effects at -12dB
