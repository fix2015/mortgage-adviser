## Bidding Strategy

### Recommended Strategy

| Setting              | Value                                               |
|----------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| Strategy             | Maximise Conversions with Target CPA                |
| Target CPA           | £8.00                                               |
| Initial Strategy     | Maximise Clicks (first 2 weeks for data collection) |
| Switch Trigger       | After 15+ conversions, switch to Target CPA         |

### Reasoning

1. **Start with Maximise Clicks** for the first 2 weeks to gather conversion data. With a £10/day budget, this ensures sufficient click volume for Google's algorithm to learn.

2. **Switch to Maximise Conversions with Target CPA of £8.00** once you have 15+ conversions. At £15 revenue per consultation, a £8 CPA delivers a 1.875x ROAS (£15 revenue / £8 cost), leaving margin for operational costs.

3. **Why £8 Target CPA:** At an average CPC of £1.50-£2.00 and expected conversion rate of 20-25% (landing page to purchase), the natural CPA should be £6-£10. Setting £8 gives the algorithm room to optimise whilst maintaining profitability.

|-------------------|--------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Affordability     | 30% (£3/day)       | High intent, strong conversion queries |
| First-Time Buyer  | 40% (£4/day)       | Largest addressable audience segment   |
| Mortgage Advice   | 30% (£3/day)       | Broad capture, brand building          |

## Landing Page Requirements

To achieve a Quality Score of 7+ (out of 10), the landing page at `https://mortgage-advisor.probooking.app/` must include:

### Above the Fold (visible without scrolling)

- [ ] **Clear headline** matching ad copy (e.g., "AI Mortgage Advice for First-Time Buyers")
- [ ] **Value proposition** — what the user gets for £15
- [ ] **Primary CTA button** — "Get Your Mortgage Advice — £15" in emerald green (#059669)
- [ ] **Trust signals** — e.g., "Trusted by X UK home buyers" or security badges
- [ ] **Price transparency** — £15 clearly displayed, no hidden costs

### Below the Fold

- [ ] **How It Works** — 3-step process (Enter details > AI analyses > Get advice)
- [ ] **What You'll Learn** — bullet list of what the consultation covers
- [ ] **FAQ section** — address common concerns (Is it regulated? How accurate? etc.)
- [ ] **Social proof** — testimonials or usage statistics
- [ ] **Security reassurance** — SSL badge, data protection statement

### Technical Requirements

- [ ] **Mobile responsive** — must work perfectly on mobile (60%+ of traffic)
- [ ] **Page load speed** — under 3 seconds (use Google PageSpeed Insights, aim for 90+)
- [ ] **SSL certificate** — HTTPS required (already in place)
- [ ] **Conversion tracking** — Google Ads conversion tag on Stripe checkout success page
- [ ] **No intrusive interstitials** — no popups that block content on mobile
- [ ] **Keyword relevance** — page content must include target keywords naturally
- [ ] **Structured data** — add FAQ schema markup for rich results
