## Email Design Guidelines
- **Template:** Clean, single-column, mobile-first
- **Width:** 600px max
- **Brand colours:** Emerald green (#059669) for CTAs, warm gold (#D97706) for accents
- **Font:** System font stack (no web fonts for deliverability)
- **CTA buttons:** Full-width on mobile, 44px min height, high contrast
- **Images:** Minimal — prioritise text for deliverability
- **Footer:** Unsubscribe link, company address, FCA disclaimer

## Deliverability Rules
- Authenticate domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Warm up sending domain gradually
- Keep bounce rate under 2%
- Keep unsubscribe rate under 0.5%
- A/B test subject lines on 10% of list before full send
- Send between 9-11am GMT (optimal UK open times)
- Never buy email lists
- Always honour unsubscribe within 24 hours

## Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open rate | >25% |
| Click rate | >4% |
| Unsubscribe rate | <0.3% |
| Bounce rate | <1% |
| Conversion rate (email → paid) | >5% |
